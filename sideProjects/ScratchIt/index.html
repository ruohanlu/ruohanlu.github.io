Scratch It!

Wanna scratch something?

Project Info:
  • Tools: Unreal5
  • Date of Completion: 2024.10
  • Controller: Mouse & Keyboard
  • Art Style: Realistic & doodle
  • Project Download Link: ScratchIt-itch.io

Introduction

First Jam in Duke. Theme is "from scratch". So we just made a game in which you scratch a lot. Try to trap the ball on the spinning circle. Left click and drag to scratch on the board. Press and hold R to restart when you messed things up and Esc to pause when you feel like it.
