Interstellar Drive

Unforgettable Co-op Experience

Interstellar Drive is a co-op road trip adventure where players play two musicians—a 1970s guitarist and a singer from the future—driving a space car, navigate surreal worlds, recruit unique band members, and create groundbreaking music to perform at the interstellar music festival.

Project Info:
  • Platform: Windows
  • Tools: Unreal5 & C++ & Blueprint
  • Controller: Mouse & Keyboard & Controller
  • Art Style: Realistic
  • Role: Technical & Design Director
  • Publisher: Voyager Studios
  • Steam Link: Voyager Studios - Interstellar Drive

What have I done

  • Led the technical team to develop Interstellar Drive, a cozy co-op driving game based on Unreal5.
  • Responsible for designing and developing the game’s game ability system. Prototype creation and iterative experience.
