Night of Full Moon

A Unity Deck-building Game

"Night of the Full Moon" is a deck-building game based on the tale of Little Red Riding Hood. You play as Red herself, battle against monsters with cards to save your beloved grandma. You can build your own deck with the cards you collect through the adventure.

Project Info:
  • Platform: Windows & Mac
  • Tools: Unity & C# & Lua & PrefDog
  • Controller: Mouse & Keyboard
  • Art Style: Cartoon & realistic
  • Role: Gameplay Programmer Intern
  • Steam Link: Night of Full Moon - Steam
  • Apple Store Link: Night of Full Moon - Mobile

What have I done

  • Used Unity Profiler and PrefDog to optimize memory strategy to reduce the heat on the mobile platform and maintain the frame rate above 50 fps through a rebuild.
  • Built VFX framework and worked with artists to implement VFX in the game through dynamic loading or preloading in Lua.
  • Implemented the game's card-scoring logic and poker hand judgment system in Lua.
  • Took charge of accessing the WeChat platform SDK to implement advertising functions.
  • Developed a duplicate asset detection tool to reduce the game package size by 52 percent.
